# Privacy Policy for Animal Dash: Kids Fun Race

**Effective Date:** June 16, 2026

Thank you for playing **Animal Dash: Kids Fun Race**! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, enjoyable experience for all players, especially children.

This Privacy Policy explains our data collection, use, and disclosure practices.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

"Animal Dash: Kids Fun Race" is designed to be a safe environment for children. The app does not require users to create an account, provide their name, email address, physical address, or any other personally identifiable information (PII).

## 2. Children's Privacy
We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Google Play Families Policy. Because our app is directed to children:
- We do not collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
- We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services that track users across other apps or websites.
- The game operates completely offline after the initial download, ensuring no data is transmitted to our servers during gameplay.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising networks that collect user data. There are no in-app purchases or external links that lead outside the app without a parental gate.

## 4. Device Permissions
The app only requests the minimum necessary permissions required for the game to function properly (such as access to the screen and device audio). We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or location data.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date". We encourage parents and guardians to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
